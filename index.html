A little about me.

For most of my adult life, I could have been considered a wanderer. I travelled around the world, in and out of school, trying to figure out what I was passionate about. When I met my now wife, I settled down and started working for Starbucks. It was through their college assistance program that I started taking some web development classes, and I fell in love with it. Things with that school didn't work out, and that's where I found Epicodus!

Outside of work.

When I am not climbing, I am either reading, playing music, or spending time playing card games with my wife. We like to walk to nearby coffee shops and play games.
